Overview

PentaGraph is a digital recreation of the iconic 5-band graphic EQ circuit found in legendary guitar amplifiers and widely recreated as standalone pedals. Rather than a generic parametric EQ with similar frequency points, PentaGraph was engineered from the ground up to reproduce the topology, interaction, and saturation behaviour of the original analog circuit.

The result is an EQ that feels like the real thing: true band independence, the characteristic "V-curve" scoop, focused cuts and boosts at extreme settings, and subtle harmonic warmth when pushed hard.

Key Features

  • Parallel Summing Topology: Each band filters the dry input independently and the results are summed, exactly as in the original analog circuit. This preserves true band independence: adjusting one slider does not affect the baseline that other bands see.
  • Hardware-Calibrated Response: All centre frequencies, Q values, and gain mappings were derived from studio measurements of real hardware. PentaGraph uses the measured values, not the nominal label values, so the plugin matches what the hardware actually does at every slider position.
  • Gain-Dependent Q: At extreme boost or cut settings, the effective Q narrows, just like the analog circuit. Deep cuts and boosts feel focused and precise rather than broad and muddy.
  • Analog Saturation Modelling: Per-band and post-summing saturation stages model the headroom characteristics of the original op-amp stages. Anti-aliased processing keeps the harmonics clean without oversampling overhead.
  • Input & Output Trim: Full-range input trim (−40 to +6 dB) lets you drive the saturation stages harder for more analog character, while the output trim provides precise level matching.
  • Zero-Latency Processing: No latency added to your signal chain. Suitable for tracking, mixing, and live use.

Controls

Control Range Description
80 Hz ±12 dB Low-end thump and sub control
240 Hz ±12 dB Low-mid body and warmth
750 Hz ±12 dB Mid focus: the classic scoop frequency
2.2 kHz ±12 dB Upper-mid attack and presence
6.6 kHz ±12 dB Presence, air, and top-end sparkle
Input Trim −40 to +6 dB Drives saturation when raised above unity
Output Trim −40 to +6 dB Final level control for gain staging

Double-click any control to reset it to 0 dB.

System Requirements

macOS

  • macOS 10.15 (Catalina) or later
  • 64-bit Intel or Apple Silicon (Universal Binary)
  • 4 GB RAM (8 GB recommended)
  • ~50 MB free disk space

Windows

  • Windows 10 (version 1903) or later, 64-bit
  • x86-64 processor; ARM64 via emulation
  • 4 GB RAM (8 GB recommended)
  • ~50 MB free disk space

Installation

macOS: Download the signed and notarised .pkg installer. Run the installer to place the VST3 and/or AU components on your system. Open your DAW and scan for new plug-ins. PentaGraph will appear under Nullform Audio.

Windows: Download the .exe installer. Choose Full, VST3 only, or Custom installation. Open your DAW and scan for new plug-ins.
